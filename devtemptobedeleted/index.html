Who we are

Made for laughs, built for fans

Luck Is For Dudes started as a pile of sketchbooks and a love of comic-book chaos. We draw our own parody-style artwork and print it on everyday stuff you actually use — because a good gag deserves more than a lonely hard drive.

Every design is made in-house. We keep the lines bold, the jokes sharp, and the colours loud enough to wake the neighbours. Perfect if you collect comics, love pop culture, or just want a mug that starts conversations.

  • In-house artwork — not random clip art
  • Printed on quality blanks you’ll want to wear and use
  • Small batches with big personality
